Details

Doors open at eight in the evening at Roche Dorée. Dinner is served after the couple's first dance, so a punctual arrival means you won't miss a moment of the celebration.

This is an alcohol-free celebration — thank you for honoring the couple's wishes.

We love your little ones, but this evening is adults-only.

Reception

The reception follows immediately at the same venue, under the garden lights of Roche Dorée. Expect an evening of dinner, dancing, and a few surprises the couple has been keeping close.

Getting There & Help

Roche Dorée sits just outside Mejdlaya; parking is available on site. If you need directions, help with your reservation, or have any questions before the day, we're one call away.
